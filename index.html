What's Inside

Everything Your Playgroup Needs

Built specifically for Commander. Every feature designed around how you actually play.

⚔️
Life Counter
Full-table life tracking with commander damage, poison counters, and death tracking for up to 6 players. Supports all major formats.
📊
Match History
Every game recorded. Search, filter, and review your entire match history with notes, duration, and final life totals.
🃏
Deck Stats
Track win rates, average game duration, and performance by player count for every deck in your collection.
👥
Saved Groups
Save your regular playgroup for one-tap game setup. Group leaderboards show who's actually running the table.

Friends System
Add friends and automatically send matches to their history. No more manual data entry for your whole playgroup.
⚡
Quickstart
Skip the setup and jump straight into a game. Pick format, pick player count, and start playing in seconds.
