Meticulous Data

Unrelenting
Truths.

Life Counter
Full-table life tracking with commander damage, poison counters, and death tracking for up to 6 players across all major formats.
Match History
Every game recorded. Search, filter, and review your complete match history with notes, duration, and final life totals.
