Account Management

Delete Your Account

You have the right to request deletion of your MTG Tracker App account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion, what gets removed, and what — if anything — is retained.

How to Request Deletion

You can delete your account directly inside the MTG Tracker App, or by contacting us via email.

  • Open the MTG Tracker App and sign in to your account
  • Tap the profile icon in the top right
  • Scroll to the bottom of the Account Settings screen and tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted — your account and data will be permanently removed

Prefer to request deletion by email? Send us a message with the subject line Account Deletion Request and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

Email hi@mtgtrackerapp.com

What Data is Deleted

Data Type Action Retention Period
Account credentials (email, password) Deleted Immediately
Display name & username Deleted Immediately
Match history Deleted Immediately
Deck data Deleted Immediately
Saved groups Deleted Immediately
Friend connections Deleted Immediately
Matches shared with friends Retained Retained in friends' histories with your name anonymized
Note: Matches that were shared with and accepted by other players will remain in those players' match histories. Your name will be anonymized in those records. All other personal data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
